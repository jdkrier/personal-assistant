# Goose

Your personal co-pilot. Opens on login, already knows your day.

Goose pulls your Canvas assignments, Google Calendar, and today's free time blocks —
then tells you exactly when to work on what. One sentence at the top. No setup.
No buttons. It just knows.

> "Do the 320 problem set between 2–4pm. Essay can wait until tonight."

Named after Goose from Top Gun. Your wingman for daily life.
